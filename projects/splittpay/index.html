SplittPay

Oct, 2013
An iPhone application that allows you to share a bill for dinner at a restaurant between your companions.

Role: iOS Developer

Team: The iOS application was developed by 3-4 developers.

Responsibility: Programming a bill recognition, programming phone book layout, programming UI.

Technologies: Objective-C, MRR, ABBY OCR

App

Often you have to pay for your friends at a pub or a restaurant and they don’t return the money after that? This application should solve your problem (actually it can’t solve it because this app is closed already).

  • Go to a bar with friends and pay for the whole company.
  • Open the application, take a photo of the bill.
  • While the photo is being recognized, you choose from your phone book those who had the good fortune to share this evening with you.

  • After the photo has been recognized (using Abby FineReader), you can see your dinner split by positions from the bill.

  • Moreover, you have the opportunity to distribute the positions of the bill among the participants of the event and see who owes you how much at the end of the fun.

  • After “splitting” the bill, you indicate the card you want to receive money on, and give the application the “Notify” command.

  • As a result, all the participants in the evening receive SMS messages with the amount of the debt and a link to the web-based card-to-card transfer form with an already substituted credit card and the amount received on their phones. They just need to enter their card details and confirm the transfer.

  • As funds are credited to the event history, you can track who has already paid their share, and who still have debt on them.
