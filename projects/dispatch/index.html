Dispatch United

Apr, 2014
An iPhone/iPad application as for independent taxi drivers so as for small taxi companies.

Role: iOS Developer

Responsibility: Developing the whole application.

Technologies: Objective-C, ARC, CoreLocation, Geo-Coding

App

Independant drivers can register and share work an info with eachother, but you can also register as a private group, it allows you to see all your drivers and their status in real time, You can assign current jobs and even create a schedule for future jobs. You can instant message the group or individual drivers separately. You can mark important information about traffic situations and locations where there are many customers.
